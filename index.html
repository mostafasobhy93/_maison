About MAISON DE LUXE

My favorite project is always the next one” – Michael Graves MAISON DE LUXE is an innovative interior design agency and project management and also specialized in all kinds of events planning company with offices in Turkey and France.

MAISON DE LUXE’s foundation in 2017 focused mainly on residential projects but has since been involved in prestigious commercial, yachting, and hospitality projects around the world.
Drawing from our backgrounds, our teams of MAISON DE LUXE designers share vast experience in the domain of design and project management, tackling each project with precision and enthusiasm. We are a multi-lingual office, this mix of backgrounds adds to the creative flair of our design work.
